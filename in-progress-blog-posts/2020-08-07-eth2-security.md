## Overview
Ethereum 2.0 requires staking. With staking comes some important decisions to ensure safety of funds and maximization of staking rewards.
- Validator key safety
- Withraw key safety
- Node up-time
  - Failover plan
  - Key redundancy
  - Remote server redundancy
